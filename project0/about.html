About this game

This is an incremental game. it features:
  • A Click Button
  • Buildings
  • Upgrades

How is it different exactly?

  • Buildings

    There is no final Building. Whenever you bought a certain amount of the currently last Building, a new one gets created, with higher cost and better revenue.

  • Cost Function

    Instead of a base cost in Money, the buildings base cost are given in seconds of production. That way, you have to wait at most that many seconds until you can buy the Building. But buying a Building with a high production is much more expensive than with a low production.

  • Inflation

    As the time to wait increments exponentially, you might have to wait decades to buy the next building. But luckily, there is inflation. It decreases the price of all Builings, which counters the exponential growth.
